About Hilda

Hilda is a young girl who lives with her mother in a cabin by the woods. She goes on numerous adventures with her deerfox Twig, and two other people, David and Frida, after she moves to Trolberg, a city surrounded by a high wall in order to keep out vicious trolls that come alive at nighttime.
